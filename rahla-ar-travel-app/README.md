## Overview

**Rahla** is a concept AR-based travel application designed to transform how tourists explore Dubai.  
The app integrates **games, challenges, and interactive exploration** to gamify tourism and encourage deeper engagement with cultural and historical landmarks.

Rahla was selected as a **semi-finalist in App Olympics 2024** hosted by Dubai Chambers (Digital Sector).

---

## Problem

Traditional tourism apps provide static information.  
Rahla aimed to:

- Increase engagement through gamification  
- Encourage exploration beyond common tourist spots  
- Create interactive storytelling experiences  
- Blend education with entertainment  

---

## Solution

Rahla proposed:

- AR-based location challenges
- Gamified reward system
- Interactive landmark storytelling
- User progress tracking
- Social/team-based exploration features
