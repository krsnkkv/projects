## Overview

"Can a Machine Dream?" was an interactive session exploring creativity at the intersection of **philosophy, art, and artificial intelligence**.

The workshop combined:
- Hands-on AI demonstrations
- Creative comparison exercises
- Accessible explanations of modern AI systems
- Audience discussion on consciousness and creativity
